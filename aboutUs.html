About us

We are a Bangkok based non-profit organization which offers mental health consultation.

We currently don't have an office.

We currently only offer english consultation services but very soon offer our services in Thai Language aswell.

The Founder

Robert Ernst: I am since 8 years in Bangkok and saw many young people struggle with mental illnesses. I am unhappy how expensive and hard the access to helping healthcare is. With CrazyBrainBangkok I want to offer supportive mental healthcare for FREE on a NGO basis. We sadly don't have the same legal status as a clinic but this enables us to go a way which is maybe more helpful for those in need of help.

If you contact us via Line there is a good change that you will be talking with me. We plan to extend our services offered in Thai Language aswell. Currently we are looking for the right people to extend our staff and volunteers.

We currently don't have an office but planning in doing so when we secured a sufficient amount of founding. However we want to be reachable for people and thus a location near to MRT/BTS will be necessary.
