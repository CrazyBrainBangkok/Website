If psychiatrists have been visited in the past they often misdiagnose or don't take the person in distress serious. Often appointments take too long to get.
Or the doctor doesn't explain the process on how they plan to treat the disorder.

Our visitors often already experienced suicide attempts and often it is just a matter of time until they are successful. Most of the times there is no hope to be healed forever. But we can help learning to live with it.

We offer those individual coaching topics:

  • Identifying, discussing and confirming the existing disorder(s). Helping you to understand the diagnosis from your previous doctors.
  • Explaining the medication strategy of psychiatrists and warning about common issues and dangers of it
  • Showing a toolset of techniques on how to handle sudden mental distress
  • Help you to receiving public healthcare in Bangkok in case you come from outside Bangkok
  • Showing you existing communities of people your age who are trying to cope in healthy and unhealthy ways
