A visit at a psychiatrist is important and can't be replaced. They are specialized on mental health and also can legally prescribe strong medication.

Common issues with receiving treatment from a psychiatrist are:

  • Hard to receive an appointment quickly enough
  • Services are too expensive
  • In case of public hospitals they don't have enough time
  • They don't explain enough
  • They don't talk about the long term treatment plan
  • Receiving the wrong diagnosis

You maybe already experienced suicide attempts and often it is just a matter of time until it takes a sad ending. Most of the times there is no hope to be healed forever. But we can help learning to live with a mental illness.

We offer those individual coaching topics:

  • Identifying, discussing and confirming the existing illness(es). Helping you to read and understand the diagnosis you received from your previous doctors.
  • Explaining the medication strategy of psychiatrists and warning about common issues and dangers of it
  • Showing a toolset of techniques on how to handle sudden mental distress
  • Help you to receiving public healthcare in Bangkok in case you come from outside Bangkok
  • Showing you communities online and offline to process the struggle

Please contact us so we can be there for you.
