Where to meet

We don't have an office location which is accessible for the public.

But face-to-face contact is important. To meet someone in real life and have a conversation. Emotions matter. Feeling being real and taken seriously matters.
So additional to our online consultation we also real life meeting ups.

We know several public accessible and safe environments to meet. Safety is important and a casual environment can ease the tension. We don't want to have a clinical environment. For mental issues we need comfortable spaces.

We discuss possible locations on an individual basis but often preferred locations are:

  • Malls
  • Cafes
  • At a park
  • At locations which are known and comfortable to the person in distress
