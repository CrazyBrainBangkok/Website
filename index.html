Our two services

1. Emergency Help a.k.a. "I'm in distress"

Emergency Help is about getting immediate help in distress. Have someone to talk to. Receiving the right kind of advice. This can be via Line or in some cases meeting in real life. This is for all the urgent cases in which regular doctors aren't available for whatever reason.

2. How to survive mental disorders a.k.a. "I need to be healed"

How to survive mental disorders is a consultation which helps you to understand what mental disorder you have and how to live with it and get it under control. This applies especially in receiving techniques in how to survive suicidal moods. This also includes how to talk with doctors and how to handle medication. We cannot replace the regular healthcare system but we can support you surviving in it.
