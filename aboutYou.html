About you

People with a mental illness may behave in strange ways or have strange thoughts. Mental illnesses can affect the day-to-day functioning of a person. Mental illnesses develop during the life of a person. People with a mental illness sometimes have problems dealing with other people or struggle with daily functioning.

The most common mental illnesses are

  • Bipolar / Manic Depressive
  • Severe Depression
  • Borderline
  • Anxiety
  • PTSD/C-PTSD
  • Adjustment disorder
  • Dysthymia / Neurotic Depressive

We made the experience that the most vulnerable people in Thai society falling into these categories

Age: Young adults from 16-30 years

Income: Cannot afford private medical care

Social: Limited social contacts or limited social understanding of mental health

  • How the Thai health system operates
  • How mental illnesses can be treated
  • How regular doctors treat mental illnesses
